Mammootty

Malayalam film actor Mammootty was born to Ismail (an agriculturist) and Fatima (a homemaker). He did his high
school (pre-degree) at Maharaja's College, Thevara and then studied law at Ernakulam Government Law College. It was
during his days at Maharajah's that he got his first role as an extra (junior artist) in Anubhavangal Paalichakal. Along
with Mohanlal, he has One of good actor in Mollywood.
