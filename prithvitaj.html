Prithviraj

Prithviraj Sukumaran was born at Thiruvananthapuram to actors Sukumaran and Mallika Sukumaran on 16 October
1982. He studied for a bachelor's degree in Information Technology at the University of Tasmania in Australia.

PRITHVIRAJ SUKUMARAN one of the leading star. He started play hero at the age of 20. He is a senior actor in
mollywood, but as per age he is a youth star. Prithvi got kerala state award for best actor at the age of 24 and he also
do some roles in bollywood & tamil films. He made his directorial debut in 2019 with Lucifer.
